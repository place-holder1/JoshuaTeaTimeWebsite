A bat wielding zombie slayer and a curious tech prodigy uncover a hidden mech in an abandoned arcade, only to discover a sinister force watching their every move.

Animayhem is a stylized cinematic trailer inspired by 80s retro arcade culture and zombie survival games. After taking shelter in an abandoned arcade, two unlikely survivors uncover much more than they bargained for.

Created by a team of 11 students at Purdue University, the project showcases collaborative work across the 1.5 minute animated short. From intense zombie encounters to an explosive mech finale, the project challenged the team to deliver ambitious storytelling and technical execution within an 8 month production timeline.

Directed by Joshua Boone, Animayhem releases on May 1st, 2026
